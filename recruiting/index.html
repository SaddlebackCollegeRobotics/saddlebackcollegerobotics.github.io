WHAT IT DOES 03 / 09

4 missions.

That means a dexterous arm, long-range driving across the desert, and a way to detect biologic life.

  • Autonomous Navigation — GPS + ArUco on ROS 2 Nav2 · Isaac ROS on Jetson next season
  • Robotic Arm Manipulation — pick up tools, operate panels, thread cables
  • Science Sampling — drill soil, on-board chemistry, look for signs of past life
  • Equipment Servicing — deliver payloads, flip switches, type on a keyboard
